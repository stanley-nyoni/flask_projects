About Dogzcreche

Dogzcreche is a place where you can add your dog and see all the dogs that are in the creche.

You can add a new dog by clicking the button below.

Add New Dog

Our Story

Dogzcreche was founded in 2021 by a group of dog lovers who wanted to create a place where dogs could be cared for and loved while their owners were away. We have a team of dedicated staff who are passionate about dogs and are committed to providing the best care for your dog.

How We Track Your Dog's Information

When you add a new dog to our creche, we store your dog's name and other important information in a secure database. This allows us to keep track of all the dogs in our care and provide you with regular updates about your dog's activities and well-being. Rest assured, your dog's information is kept confidential and is used solely for the purpose of providing the best care possible.

Advantages of Using Our App

  • Ease of Use: Our app is user-friendly, making it easy for you to add your dog, update information, and check on your dog's status.

  • Real-Time Updates: Get instant updates about your dog's activities and well-being.

  • Secure Data Storage: We store your dog's information in a secure database, ensuring confidentiality and privacy.

  • Personalized Care Plans: Based on the information you provide, we create a personalized care plan for your dog.
